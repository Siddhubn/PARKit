Purpose

The purpose of creating this Car Parking Reservation System is to streamline the parking experience for users by offering a platform that allows easy reservations. We aim to reduce the time and effort spent on finding parking spaces, especially in busy areas.

Our Vision and Outcome

Through this project, we aim to develop a fully functional web application that provides a seamless and user-friendly way to reserve parking slots. The outcome of creating this webpage is to:

  • Offer an accessible reservation system for parking.
  • Provide real-time updates on parking availability.
  • Enhance our skills in web development, project management, database management.
  • Contribute to solving a common issue faced by daily commuters and visitors.

We hope that this project serves as a meaningful step towards utilizing technology for practical and helpful solutions.
